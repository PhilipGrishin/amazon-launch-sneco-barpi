## Public Repository Rule

## Project Objective

Build a public-safe Amazon launch strategy workspace that helps decide which product should launch first, what data is still missing, what compliance and economic risks must be resolved, and what launch assets are required before execution.

The project should support clear decisions without exposing confidential business data.

## Launch Principles

- Compliance comes before listing preparation and launch execution.
- Unit economics must be reviewed before selecting a product for launch.
- Product selection should be based on evidence, not preference alone.
- Competitor research should use public information only.
- Listing drafts must avoid unsupported claims.
- Public repository content must stay safe to share externally.
- Decisions must be logged with a clear status and rationale.
- SnEco and TM Barpi must be evaluated separately until a launch priority is approved.

## Priority Workflow

1. Collect data.
2. Check category and compliance.
3. Calculate unit economics.
4. Analyze competitors.
5. Select first product.
6. Prepare listing.
7. Prepare launch plan.
8. Prepare PPC.

## Decision Statuses

- Reject
- Research deeper
- Prepare launch
- Ready for execution

## Governance Rules

- No listing can be prepared for launch before compliance review and unit economics review.
- Claude outputs are drafts only and must be reviewed by ChatGPT before decisions.
- Codex maintains repository structure and file hygiene, but strategic decisions must be recorded in the decisions log.
- Any sensitive commercial input must be stored outside this public repository.
