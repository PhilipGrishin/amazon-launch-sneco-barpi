# Workflow Rules

## Public Repository Safety

- Do not store confidential business data.
- Use placeholders for prices, costs, suppliers, formulas, and private documents.
- Keep credentials and tokens out of the repository.

## File Updates

- Add sources for public research where appropriate.
- Mark assumptions clearly.
- Move decisions into the decisions log once reviewed.

## Decision Process

Use the following statuses:

- Proposed
- Under Review
- Approved
- Rejected
- Deferred
- Needs Data

## Brand Spelling

Always use `TM Barpi`.
