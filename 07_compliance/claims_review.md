Do not use unsupported health, safety, environmental, nutrition, award, origin, or performance claims in Amazon listing copy.

## Preliminary EU Food Information Finding

EU Regulation 1169/2011 requires food information not to mislead consumers regarding characteristics such as nature, identity, properties, composition, quantity, durability, country of origin/provenance, and method of manufacture/production.

For distance selling, required food information should be available before purchase except date of minimum durability/use-by. Mandatory food information should appear in a language easily understood by consumers in the Member State where the food is marketed.
