# Claims Review

## Public Repository Rule

This repository is public. Do not include confidential costs, supplier data, private documents, personal data, credentials, or non-public commercial strategy.

## Active Scope

Only SnEco Cheddar 28g EN and SnEco Gouda 28g EN are active for the current Amazon Europe claims review.

Do not use Slovak front packaging, Ukrainian packaging, other cheese variants, nut mixes, or TM Barpi in the current active SnEco scope.

## Rule

Evidence availability does not automatically mean the claim is approved for Amazon listing use. All claims must be checked against Amazon policy and EU marketplace requirements before publication.

Do not use unsupported health, safety, environmental, nutrition, award, origin, or performance claims in Amazon listing copy.
