## First Amazon Europe Compliance Findings

### EU Food Information Rules

EU Regulation 1169/2011 requires food information not to mislead consumers regarding characteristics such as nature, identity, properties, composition, quantity, durability, country of origin/provenance, and method of manufacture/production.

For distance selling, required food information should be available before purchase except date of minimum durability/use-by. Mandatory food information should appear in a language easily understood by consumers in the Member State where the food is marketed.

### Nutrition

The current nutrition declaration contains the key mandatory nutrition elements: energy, fat, saturates, carbohydrate, sugars, protein, and salt.

Nutrition data is provided per 100 g, which matches EU nutrition declaration logic.

### Allergen

Required Amazon data point: Contains: Milk.

Milk must be treated as a critical allergen disclosure because EU Regulation 1169/2011 lists milk and products thereof, including lactose, as an allergen category.

### High Protein

EU Regulation 1924/2006 allows "high protein" where at least 20% of the energy value of the food is provided by protein.

Preliminary calculations:

| SnEco Gouda 28g EN | 48.3 g protein x 4 kcal = 193.2 kcal from protein; 193.2 / 625 kcal | About 30.9% of energy from protein | Potentially usable after nutrition value verification and Amazon listing wording review |

### Claims And Listing Wording

- Keto friendly: do not use in Amazon listing at first launch. Keep as packaging claim only until separate compliance decision.
- Healthy crunchy supersnack: not recommended for Amazon listing. Avoid in title, bullets, A+, and backend content.
- From 100% Cheddar/Gouda Cheese: evidence is available through composition/specification, but exact Amazon wording must be reviewed.
- Preferred safer listing wording: "Made from Cheddar cheese", "Made from Gouda cheese", or "Crunchy bites made from cheese".
- Avoid over-broad wording such as "100% pure cheese" until reviewed.
- Produced utilizing renewable energy: evidence available through solar panels, but do not make it a main listing claim at first launch. Use only after evidence file and wording review.
- SIAL Innovation Paris 2024 Grand Prix: certificate available. Potentially usable after exact certificate wording and logo/award-use rights are verified.
